## **1.1 What Is Physical AI?**

| **Feature** | **Description** |
|-------------|----------------|
| **Embodied Intelligence** | AI integrated with hardware bodies |
| **Interaction with the Physical World** | Sensing, acting, adapting |
| **Autonomous Behavior** | Decision-making without constant human input |
| **Learning from Experience** | Reinforcement learning, imitation learning, adaptive control |

## **1.2 What Are Humanoid Robots?**

| **Aspect** | **Details** |
|------------|-------------|
| **Shape** | Head, torso, arms, legs |
| **Motion** | Human-like gestures and locomotion |
| **Applications** | Household, industrial, caregiving, social interaction |
